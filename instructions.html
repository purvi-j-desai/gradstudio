Instructions

The GradStudio Project is a free, open online project you can join to give and receive feedback on your graduate application essays. To join the Peer Review Essay Project, you should have a full and complete draft of your application essay, either a personal statement, statement of purpose, or a research statement ("what is a research statement?"). This does not have to be a final or "polished" essay, but should have a beginning, middle, and end in order for you to get the most out of the project. Please be sure you title your essay as either a research statement or statement of purpose/personal statement so that your reviewers will know how to give you the best feedback.

  1. Fill out the survey on the front page of The GradStudio Project
  2. Sign up for an account on PeerStudio
  3. Once you have an account, return to the GradStudio Page and click “Submit My Essay” to be taken to your assignment in PeerStudio
  4. In the PeerStudio assignment, you will be able to upload your essay for peer review
  5. You will then be prompted to review 2 other graduate application essays. Instructions for reviewing will be provided. Be a thoughtful and helpful reviewer!
  6. Once you have completed the required peer review and others have reviewed your essay, you will have the option to view the feedback on your essay.
  7. To complete the project, you will submit your essay with revisions to get another round of useful feedback!
  8. The deadline for the entire project, including both submissions, is December 1st! Be sure to complete all your peer reviews by this date to get the most benefit from the project.

Got lost? Need help? Check out the FAQs. If your question isn’t answered there, email GradStudioProject@gmail.com
