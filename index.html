Welcome to the GradStudio Project!

The GradStudio Project uses peer evaluation to give you quick feedback for improving your graduate application essays. When you join the project, you become part of a large, diverse group of participants who have all agreed to share and give feedback on each others' essays.

The project is open to any interested applicants currently working on application essays for graduate programs. The deadline for completing the project is December 1st. After this date, the project will close.

To join the Peer Review Essay Project, you should have a full and complete draft of your application essay, either a personal statement, statement of purpose, or a research statement (what is a research statement?). This does not have to be a final or "polished" essay, but should have a beginning, middle, and end in order for you to get the most out of the project. Please be sure you title your essay as either a research statement or statement of purpose/personal statement so that your reviewers will know how to give you the best feedback.
