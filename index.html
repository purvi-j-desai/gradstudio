The GradStudio Project!

Revision Help for Application Essays

The GradStudio Project uses peer evaluation to give you quick feedback for improving your graduate application essays. When you join the project, you become part of a large, diverse group of participants who have all agreed to share and give feedback on each others' essays.

The project is open to any interested applicants currently working on application essays for graduate programs. It will be an ongoing process: over the course of the next month, you will go through two rounds of feedback and revision of your essay. The project will close on December 1st.

To join the Peer Review Essay Project, you should have a full and complete draft of your application essay, either a personal statement, statement of purpose, or a research statement (what is a research statement?). This does not have to be a final or "polished" essay, but should have a beginning, middle, and end in order for you to get the most out of the project.
